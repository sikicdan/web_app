About Us

Travel CANADA

Travel CANADA offers a broad assortment of fabulous Canadian vacation packages, all intended to make memories that will last for a lifetime. We offer better tours at better prices and have done all the planning for you so you can maximize your time to sit back and enjoy your well earned relaxation. Our tours combine spectacular train travel and accommodations with exciting tours and activities across Canada and the Canadian Rockies.

Have a look through our train trips to get some ideas, then give us a call, send us an email or book online. We are your best choice for expert local travel advice and reservations for the Canadian Rockies and we would love to help you make arrangements in the rest of Canada as well.

Our One-Way Tours run through the provinces of British Columbia and Alberta showcasing the beautiful Canadian Rocky Mountains. These tours run in either direction - eastbound (starting in Vancouver) or westbound (starting in Calgary/ Banff). We offer a variety of itineraries to suit your ideal vacation length, each departing two or three times per week. If there is something you would like to add to one of our vacation packages, please contact us and we will be happy to customize the tour to make it exactly what you are looking for.

Circle Tours are a great way to experience more of Western Canada as they allows you to travel on different train routes and enjoy many excursions along the way. These itineraries can also be extended so you can relax in the resort towns on your own schedule. Should you wish to customize any of these vacation packages please contact us and let us know your wishes. We are committed to providing the best tour experience as we live right here in Canada and we know the best ways to maximize your enjoyment.
